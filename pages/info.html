Focus on Jinx

Passive: Get Excited!

Whenever Jinx assists in or scores a takedown on an enemy champion, turret, or inhibitor, she gains a massive boost of movement and attack speed for a few seconds.

Q: Switcheroo!

Jinx swaps her weapons, gaining increased attack speed with her minigun and dealing bonus damage with her rocket launcher.

W: Zap!

Jinx fires a shock blast that damages and slows the first enemy hit.

E: Flame Chompers!

Jinx throws out a line of snare grenades that explode after a brief delay, rooting enemies that walk over them.

R: Super Mega Death Rocket!

Jinx fires a global rocket that gains damage as it travels. It explodes on the first enemy champion hit, dealing damage to the target and surrounding enemies based on their missing health.
