JINX

The Loose Cannon

Unhinged and impulsive criminal from the undercity, Jinx is haunted by the consequences of her past. But that never stops her from bringing her own brand of chaos to Piltover and Zaun. She uses her homemade arsenal in devastating ways, unleashing torrents of explosions and colorful gunfire, and the havoc she leaves behind often inspires the downtrodden to rebel and resist.
